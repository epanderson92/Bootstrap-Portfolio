    Bio

    Now Ya Know

    My name is Evan Anderson and I am musician. My primary instrument is guitar, which I have been playing for 10 years. I recently moved back to Austin, Texas from Northern California where I was able to intern in a studio called Sound Garage. I also was able to play bass in a jazz/blues band who got to open for Pat Travers.

    In addition to playing music I like to study Spanish and Swedish, stand up paddle board, eat pizza and physical activity. I also like to travel. The only place I've been outside of the counrty is Romania, which was a cool experience. Some tours I've been on have brough me to South Carolina, North Carolina and Florida.

    I recently got the opportunity to work for a home remodeling company for the past 8 months called "We Do Home Improvements". I got to learn many interesting trade skills like painting, dry wall repair, sub-flooring, and more.
